08 — Business model How it makes money

Free to acquire. Paid to automate. Cheap to run.

Free — the wedge

The full on-device organizer. ~$0 marginal cost. No account. This is the acquisition engine.

Paid — automatic handling

~$20–30 / year for fully passive organization. The proven, sustainable band — and an undercut of the weekly-priced cleaners.

Revenue comes from convenience, not from your data. Run cost stays near zero because the work is on-device.
