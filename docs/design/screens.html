What you're judging here is restraint, not features. No rounded blobby cards, no shadows on content, no chrome competing with the images. The only typography that isn't the photos: a hairline wordmark, a quiet name, and the count in monospace — the one Nuke tell. The single moment of motion in the whole app is the blur resolving in and out. If this feels simple, perfect, calm, it's right.
