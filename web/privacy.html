Privacy Policy

Last updated: <SET DATE ON PUBLISH>

Blur collects nothing. Everything it does happens on your device, and no data ever leaves your phone.

What we collect

Nothing. Blur does not collect, transmit, store on our servers, or share any personal data. It has no user accounts and makes no network requests in normal use. You can use it in airplane mode.

Photo access

Blur asks for access to your photo library so it can organize it into galleries and let you hide photos. All of this happens entirely on your device. Your photos are never uploaded, copied off the device, or sent to us or any third party.

On-device data

Your choices in Blur — such as which photos you hide — are stored only on your device. They are removed when you delete the app. We never receive them.

No tracking, no third parties

Blur contains no advertising, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party trackers. We do not profile you or build any record of your activity.

Children

Blur is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages. Because it collects no data, it collects no data from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date. If Blur ever adds an optional feature that processes data off-device, this policy will be updated to describe it in plain terms before that feature ships, and any such processing will be opt-in.
